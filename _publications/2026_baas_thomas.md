**ARTICLE**

Authors: Natasha Baas-Thomas, Abuzar Mahmood, Narendra Mukherjee, Kathleen C. Maigler, Yixi Wang, Donald B. Katz

<b>ABSTRACT:</b>
This study links gustatory cortical neural dynamics to the physical movements that define ingestive behavior. Using jaw-opener EMG, gustatory cortical ensemble recordings, and a machine-learning behavioral classifier, the work identifies ingestion-related movement subtypes and shows that their changing frequencies follow the cortical transition into a hedonic-value state.

<b>ABSTRACT:</b>
This study investigates a novel needle-free therapeutic micromist delivery system carrying Tumor Necrosis Factor (TNF) silencer RNA for treating chronic wounds. The research demonstrates the effectiveness of this approach in expediting chronic wound healing, repairing deep muscle damage, and improving survival in a new rat model.
